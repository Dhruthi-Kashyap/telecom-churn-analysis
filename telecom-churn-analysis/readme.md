#  Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

**Tools:** Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn

---

##  Project Overview
Analyzed 7,043 telecom customer records to identify 
key churn drivers and high-risk customer segments. 
Built a multi-chart dashboard to visualize findings 
and provided actionable business recommendations.

---

##  Key Insights

1. **Overall churn rate is 26.58%** — 1 in 4 customers 
   are leaving the company

2. **Month-to-month contracts churn at 42%** — 15x higher 
   than 2-year contract customers (2.83%)

3. **First 18 months is the danger zone** — churned customers 
   have avg tenure of 18 months vs 37 months for loyal customers

4. **Fiber optic customers churn at 42%** — double the rate 
   of DSL users despite paying premium prices

5. **Electronic check users churn the most** — auto payment 
   customers are the most loyal

---

##  Business Impact
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Customers Analysed | 7,043 |

| Est. Annual Revenue Lost | $1.4M |

##  Business Recommendations
1. Launch loyalty program in **first 18 months** to retain new customers
2. Offer discounts to move customers from **monthly to annual contracts**
3. Investigate **fiber optic service quality** and introduce retention pricing
4. Introduce loyalty discounts for customers paying **above $70/month**
